How do I book an appointment? ▾
Click “Make Appointment” on the homepage and sign in (or create an account). Choose a date, time, and service—done in under a minute.
What should I bring? ▾
A valid ID and any relevant medical records or prescriptions. For new patients, please arrive 10 minutes early.
Can I reschedule? ▾
Yes. You can cancel or reschedule from your account page, subject to availability.
